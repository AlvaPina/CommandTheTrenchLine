Game Description

In Command the Trench Line, you control and position platoons to capture and defend trenches. The trenches offer protection. Furthermore, there are different types of troops you can combine to achieve victory. Do you have what it takes to destroy the enemy base?

How To Play

Select which troops you want to take with you into battle. Each troop has its advantages and disadvantages.

Using the arrow keys and the numbers of your keyboard, you can select your armies. Once an army is selected, use the arrows to move left and right or the space bar to order it to retreat.

For winning you need to destroy de enemy base. It wont be easy, there will be other enemy armies. For killing them, just get close them.
