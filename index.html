Game Description

In Command the Trench Line, you control and position platoons to capture and defend trenches. In future updates, you'll need to manage resources like coins, materials, and soldiers. For the Beta, you'll be carefully choosing the right troops to engage the enemy, knowing how to combine and move them appropriately. Kill all the enemies for victory!

How To Play

Due to the wide variety of troop types we have, it will take you a while to choose xd. Now seriously, in future updates, there will be more troops that make more things.

Using the arrow keys and the numbers of your keyboard, you can move your armies. If for example you want to move the army number 3, you just need to click number 3 on your keyboard and then user the arrows to move.

For winning you need to kill all enemy armies. For killing enemy armies you just need to get close to them. In the future there will be another mode for killing the team base for winning and war map and resources!
